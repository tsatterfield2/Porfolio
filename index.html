About Myself

I'm a dedicated developer based in Lincoln, Nebraska, with a degree in Computer Science bringing a wealth of experience and a continuous love for learning. I've gained valuable work experience that complements my passion for crafting clean and efficient code. I'm committed to designing simple and visually appealing user interfaces, prioritizing ease of navigation. While I initially pursued a degree in Computer Science at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, my journey has expanded beyond academia, and I'm now actively seeking opportunities to contribute my skills and expertise. Here are some of my key skills:

Static/Responsive Websites

I craft static websites using the fundamental trio of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. By integrating jQuery, I add seamless transitions to enhance the overall user experience, giving the pages a polished feel. I prioritize making these websites responsive to diverse screen sizes, ensuring a consistently appealing display across various devices. Through a combination of clean design principles and practical functionality, I aim to deliver static websites that not only look good but also provide a user-friendly experience for visitors.

Full-Stack Development

I'm experienced in Full-Stack Development, with a strong grasp of both front-end and back-end technologies. I'm particularly proficient in Angular for creating dynamic and responsive user interfaces, and I also have solid expertise in C# .NET for building robust server-side applications. My Full-Stack capabilities enable me to develop end-to-end solutions, connecting the user interface with the server to deliver comprehensive and functional software.

DevOps

I'm skilled in DevOps, making software development and operations work together smoothly. I know how to use tools like Jenkins, Git, and Azure DevOps to automate tasks and improve efficiency. My expertise includes managing code, setting up pipelines/builds, and keeping an eye on performance. I focus on teamwork and communication to make sure software is delivered quickly and works well.
